What You Can Do

Your photo library,
finally on the map.

TravelView reads GPS data from your photos and puts them exactly where they belong — no manual entry, no hassle.

Photo Map View

Every photo with GPS metadata appears as a circular pin on the map. Tap to view, edit, or navigate to that location.

Custom Albums

Create geo-located albums for any trip, event, or place. Add your photos, set a location, and see the whole story on the map.

Edit Locations

Missing GPS data? No problem. Manually add or correct any location by typing a place name or coordinates. Updates instantly on the map.

Yearly Albums

Automatically organized year-by-year. See exactly what happened in 2023, 2024, or any year — and where in the world you were.

Swipe Navigation

Browse photos in any album with intuitive swipe gestures. Smooth animations, circular navigation, and full-quality display.

Stays Private

Everything runs on your device. No cloud uploads, no accounts, no data leaving your phone. Your photos stay yours.
